# Forgotten Domain: Wyvvrstvrm — Game Design Document

## Core Story

In 2926, Earth was ravaged by a brutal World War that corporations ultimately won. With governments dissolved, the megacorps launched **Project Harmony** — a secret fusion of quantum science and ancient alchemy meant to create a perfectly controlled society. Instead, they tore open **The Fracture**, a catastrophic rift that shattered reality itself.

One hundred years later in 3026, humanity now exists across five fractured worlds. Every citizen can still look up and see the broken remains of Earth hanging in their sky like a dead moon.

## The Five Planets

### Silith-9 — The False Moon (silver)
When the Fracture occurred, nine of the most powerful corporate bloodlines escaped together and claimed the largest surviving fragment, naming it Silith-9. It has become a cold, sterile technocracy of towering orbital spires and machine cities. Its rulers view themselves as the only true humans left. Their secret agenda is to study the other four planets, reverse-engineering their magic, biology, and physics to map and ultimately control The Fracture. Their creatures are mechanical, cybernetic, and genetically engineered horrors.

### Orilyth — The Veil of Whispers (blue)
Long before the Fracture, the people of Orilyth foresaw Earth's doom and sent countless warnings that were suppressed by the elites. Now, from their realm of energy storms and crystalline spires, they act as the information network of the fractured universe. They constantly warn others about Silith-9 (calling it The False Moon) and expose its plan to harvest energy from all planets.

### Sanguis — The Crimson Veil (red)
Widely believed to be a violent war world, Sanguis is actually the most peaceful and spiritually balanced planet. Its terrifying crimson storms and magma creatures are purely defensive. Orilyth gifted them advanced technology, including a space laboratory. Silith-9 spreads propaganda claiming Sanguis is evil.

### Voxya — The Hollow Echo (black)
A world of perpetual darkness and silence. Its people are quiet, introspective survivors suffering from deep existential melancholy, constantly searching for meaning in a broken universe. They are mostly neutral but quietly shelter Orilyth refugees and receive visits from Sanguis healers.

### Korvess — The Verdant Abyss (green)
A savage, hyper-evolving jungle world where life mutates at terrifying speed. The outer regions are extremely dangerous. Its people live in symbiosis with this violent ecosystem and value strength and survival. Their creatures are rapidly mutating bio-horrors.

## Core Gameplay Systems

- Fully time-based gameplay (no traditional turns)
- Each player has a King base with **8000 Life Points**
- 3 action slots (must craft a portal/sigil first, then summon)
- Units have time-based movement across the board

## Currencies

### Shards
Color-specific mana. Players start with a batch of their own color's Shards.

### Universal Resources (5 types, found on all planets)
- **Nexium**
- **Vortite**
- **Chronite**
- **Helixium**
- **Voidsteel**

## Resource System

- Players start with a small amount of each resource + their color's Shards
- Resources are grown by upgrading your King base
- Players can invade other planets and capture territory to generate more resources over time
- Both Shards and Resources are required to cast spells and summon creatures
